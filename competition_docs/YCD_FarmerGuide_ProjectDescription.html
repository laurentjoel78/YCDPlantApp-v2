Executive Summary

YCD Farmer Guide is a comprehensive mobile application designed to transform smallholder farming in Cameroon. By leveraging artificial intelligence, real-time data analytics, and community-driven knowledge sharing, we provide farmers with the tools they need to increase productivity, reduce crop losses, and access fair markets‚Äîall from their smartphones.

The Challenge

Cameroon's agricultural sector employs over 70% of the rural population, yet farmers face persistent challenges: plant diseases destroy up to 40% of annual harvests, climate unpredictability disrupts planting cycles, and lack of market access forces farmers to sell through exploitative middlemen. The knowledge gap between research institutions and farmers in the field remains vast, with most agricultural extension services unable to reach remote communities.

Our Innovation

YCD Farmer Guide bridges the gap between advanced agricultural science and practical field application through an intuitive, multilingual mobile platform. Our AI-powered disease detection system uses computer vision to identify plant diseases from smartphone photos with 95% accuracy, providing instant treatment recommendations. The platform integrates real-time weather data, expert consultation booking, a peer-to-peer marketplace, and community forums‚Äîcreating a complete digital ecosystem for agricultural success.
