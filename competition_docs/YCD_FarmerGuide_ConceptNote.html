Executive Summary

YCD Farmer Guide is a mobile application that empowers smallholder farmers in Cameroon with AI-driven plant disease diagnosis, hyperlocal weather advisories, access to agricultural experts, and a marketplace for essential farm inputs and buyer connections. The platform is lightweight, bilingual (French & English), and optimized for basic Android devices.
