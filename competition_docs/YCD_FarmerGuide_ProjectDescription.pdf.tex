\vspace{1em}
\textbf{1. Project Overview}

YCD Farmer Guide is a comprehensive mobile application that serves as a digital companion for smallholder farmers in Cameroon. The platform integrates artificial intelligence, e-commerce, expert advisory services, and community features into a single, user-friendly application accessible on basic Android smartphones.

\textbf{Vision:} To become the leading digital agricultural platform in Central Africa, empowering 1 million farmers with technology-driven solutions by 2030.

\textbf{Mission:} To bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern agricultural technology, enabling smallholder farmers to increase productivity, access fair markets, and build resilient livelihoods.

\textbf{2. Detailed Feature Description}

\textbf{AI-Powered Disease Detection}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Farmers take a photo of the affected plant
  \item AI analyzes the image and provides instant diagnosis with confidence score
  \item Treatment recommendations and preventive measures in French and English
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Agricultural Marketplace}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item List and sell produce, seeds, fertilizers, and equipment
  \item Direct orders from buyers, real-time pricing, secure mobile payments
  \item Track sales and manage inventory
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Expert Advisory System}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item On-demand chat/video consultations with certified agronomists
  \item Appointment booking and pay-per-consultation model
  \item Farm visit requests for comprehensive assessment
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Community Forums}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Peer-to-peer knowledge sharing and support
  \item Region-specific discussions and best practices
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Weather Intelligence}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Hyperlocal 7-day forecasts with agricultural advisories
  \item Climate risk alerts for better planning
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Technology Stack}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item React Native (Expo), Node.js, PostgreSQL, Groq AI, Railway Cloud
\end{itemize}

\textbf{3. Impact and Scalability}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Target: 50,000+ farmers in 10 regions
  \item Crop loss reduction: 30\%
  \item Income increase: up to 2x
  \item Sustainable business model: freemium app, transaction fees, partnerships
  \item SDG alignment: No Poverty, Zero Hunger, Decent Work, Innovation
\end{itemize}
